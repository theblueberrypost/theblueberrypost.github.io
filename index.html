Perfume DIY:  Lush’s Rose Jam

August 9, 2021

Obsessed with Lush’s Rose Jam scent? Try out this super simple, alcohol-free dupe for a fraction of the price.

What you’ll need for a 10 ml bottle:

  • ⁃ 20 drops rose essential oil
  • ⁃ 20 drops vanilla absolute oil
  • ⁃ 20 drops orange (or lemon) essential oil
  • ⁃ ~7 ml fractionated coconut oil (or any other carrier oil you prefer)

oils

roll-on bottle

Add the above ingredients to a roll-on bottle and shake. Simple as that and enjoy.
